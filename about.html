Key Experiences

During my time in the Navy, I developed a diverse technical background. I spent five years working in Visual Landing Aids (VLA), where I managed systems like the Integrated Launch and Recovery Television Surveillance System (ILARTS) and the Improved Fresnel Lens Optical Landing System (IFLOLS). My role in this capacity focused heavily on information and communication technologies essential for aviation support. Additionally, I spent a year in armed security, where I ensured the safety and security of critical Navy facilities. Currently, I work in the security department of a Navy clinic, where I maintain and monitor Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS), guaranteeing the operational integrity and compliance of these critical systems.
